Our Story

A Farm Built on
Passion & Tradition

Raphael Dairies was born from a deep love of the land and animals. What began as a modest herd of dairy cattle has grown into a thriving multi-breed operation nestled in the rolling highlands, where clean air, lush pastures, and ethical farming practices come together.

We believe that healthy, happy animals produce the finest milk, wool, and meat. Every decision we make — from breeding programmes to feeding schedules — is guided by that principle. Our farm is not just a business; it is a calling.

Our prized dairy herd produces rich, creamy milk year-round. Raised on open pastures with a carefully balanced diet, they yield milk with exceptional nutritional value — the foundation of all our dairy products.

Our Products

Pure Dairy,
Crafted with Care

From our paddocks to your plate, every product that leaves Raphael Dairies carries the hallmark of quality, freshness, and ethical production.

🥛
Fresh Whole Milk

Collected daily from our mixed herd of cattle and goats. No additives, no preservatives — just pure, farm-fresh milk as nature intended.

🧀
Artisan Cheeses

Hand-crafted in small batches using traditional methods. Our Merino sheep milk pecorino and creamy goat feta are seasonal highlights.

🧈
Cultured Butter & Yoghurt

Slow-cultured for depth of flavour. Our yoghurt is thick, probiotic-rich, and perfect for every meal of the day.

🧶
Merino Wool

Hand-sheared annually, our Merino fleece is ethically processed and available in raw or spun form — ultra-fine, soft, and highly sought after.
