Dairy Breed

Dairy Goats

Our dairy goat herd produces high-quality milk used for artisan cheese, yoghurt, and other dairy products. Naturally homogenised and easier to digest than cow's milk, goat milk is increasingly valued by health-conscious consumers.

Rich Milk Production

They require good feeding and clean environments for peak milk yield throughout the season.

Artisan Dairy Products

Ideal for soft cheeses, kefir, and yoghurt — all crafted fresh on the farm.

Proper nutrition is the cornerstone of every healthy, productive goat. At Raphael Dairies, we design feeding programmes tailored to each breed — ensuring optimal growth, reproductive health, and milk quality across the entire herd.
