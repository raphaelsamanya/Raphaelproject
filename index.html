Below are Aubrac breed

The Aubrac breed is a strong and hardy cattle breed originally from France. It is well known for its ability to survive in tough weather conditions and poor grazing areas. Aubrac cows have a light brown coat, black markings around the eyes, nose, and legs, and strong horns. They are mainly raised for beef production because of their high-quality meat, but they can also produce milk for their calves. Farmers like Aubrac cattle because they are easy to manage, resistant to disease, and adapt well to different environments, making them valuable in livestock farming.
