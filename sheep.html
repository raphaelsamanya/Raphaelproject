Dorper Sheep

Dorper sheep adapt well to dry regions and provide reliable meat production.

They resist diseases and thrive in different climates with minimal care.

Known for high-quality meat and low maintenance farming.

Merino Sheep

Merino sheep are known for producing high-quality wool used in textiles.

They are valuable for both wool production and adaptability.
